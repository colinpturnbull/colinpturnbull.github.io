Habitat Home Design

2014 - 2015, Student Work

BACKGROUNDHabitat Home Design is a fictional home architecture firm. It specializes in the design of modernist style residential spaces constructed out of sustainable and eco-friendly materials. Habitat's main goal is to establish a connection between the home and the environment that surrounds it, relecting the philosophy of Frank Lloyd Wright, who believed nature and architecture should co-exist peacefully.

OBJECTIVEThe goal for this project was to create a cohesive brand identity for Habitat, including a logo, collateral, and a website, with consistent visual elements across all applications. The color scheme of green, white, and gray abstractly represents the harmony between nature and technology. The repeating triangle pattern conveys stability and trust, since the triangle is the strongest form in architecture. It is also a representation of the biological cell, the building block of all living things. The overall modern and minimal branding reflects the style of homes designed by Habitat.
