ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Colin. Born and raised in Springfield, Missouri. I am a graduate in Graphic Design from Evangel University, now my favorite thing to do is utilize my skills learned in new media. I’ve been experimenting with design as far back as I can remember, back when I drew car and snowboard designs on every surface I could as a child. From then on, all through my high school and college years, I’ve been working on logos, posters, layouts, packaging and pretty much everything that involves creating a visual representation of an idea.I enjoy working with others to see their ideas brought to life. I apply a clean, rational approach to design, so that the message is conveyed in a clear and direct manner. I know I’m finished with a project when there is nothing more to add or subtract. I am currently in the web design track at Startup Institute in Boston, where I am developing the skills, mindset, and network necessary to be high-impact in a high-growth company. I appreciate you taking the time to look at my work, let me know if you'd like to work together!
